## What Judges Will See

1. Send "HELP" on WhatsApp → bot responds
2. Share location → bot finds nearest trauma-capable hospital
3. Answer severity questions → bot dispatches services
4. First aid guidance → one step at a time
5. Voice notes in Hindi → bot responds in Hindi (text + voice)
6. Golden Hour countdown → urgency visualization

## Demo Flow

```
1. Open WhatsApp, send "HELP"
2. Bot: "What happened? Share location"
3. Send location pin
4. Bot: "How bad are injuries? Conscious?"
5. Reply "unconscious, bleeding from head"
6. Bot: dispatches nearest Level 1 trauma centre with Golden Hour
7. Bot: first aid instructions (one at a time)
8. Reply "ambulance arrived"
9. Bot: "Help has arrived. Stay safe."
```
